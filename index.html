Campus Life


Computer Lab

The school has well-equipped and well-ventilated Computer laboratories, catering to classes IX-XII students for their practical classes. With ample space and scope for work at the laboratories, students enjoy science to the hilt.

School playground

Provision for games and sports are there in the school for which well equipped playground and sports materials are available. To create an atmosphere of healthy competition and team spirit, every year inter house competitions are held for the students in the CCA periods.
